Akari Glow

Light every square with calm logic.
Offline puzzle game · 50 levels · Star ratings · Hints and undo

A quiet logic challenge

Akari Glow is a relaxing puzzle game inspired by the classic Light Up puzzle. Place bulbs so every open square is illuminated, follow numbered block clues, and avoid bulbs shining directly at each other.

Each level rewards clean deduction instead of speed. It is easy to learn, satisfying to master, and built for short thoughtful sessions.

Light the boardUse bulbs to illuminate every open square across the grid.
Follow the cluesNumbered blocks show exactly how many bulbs must touch them.
Play offlineNo account required. Progress and stars are saved locally.
