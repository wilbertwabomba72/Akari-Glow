Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Akari Glow handles information when you use the app. If you have any questions, contact us at 508923216@qq.com.

Overview

Akari Glow is a single-player puzzle game. The game can be played offline, does not require an account, and stores game progress locally on your device.

Information We Store Locally

The app may store the following information on your device:

  • Level progress
  • Star ratings
  • Music and sound effect settings
  • Basic gameplay state needed to continue playing

This local game data is used only to provide gameplay features and is not used to personally identify you.

Advertising and Device Identifiers

Akari Glow may show ads. Advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, approximate location derived from network information, and diagnostic data to load, measure, and improve ads.

On iOS, the app may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before accessing the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA). If you decline tracking permission, ads may still be shown, but they may be less personalized.

Network Use

The game itself is designed for offline play. However, the app may connect to the internet to request ad configuration, load ads, perform advertising measurement, and support security or anti-abuse SDK features included in the app.

Third-Party Services

The app may include third-party advertising, analytics, security, or measurement SDKs. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies. We do not sell personal information directly.

Children's Privacy

Akari Glow is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete it, reset app data, or uninstall the app. Data processed by advertising or security partners is retained according to their own policies.

Your Choices

You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also delete local game data by removing the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
